Ipsum Vinoteca is conveniently located in the thriving art district in the city centre, next to the BBC Leeds, the Leeds College of Music and the Northern Ballet Dance Theatre and opposite to the new John Lewis and Victoria Quarter, the Leeds City Bus Station and Leeds Kirkgate Market. Ipsum Vinoteca is  a very short distance from the West Yorkshire Playhouse and the IBIS hotel.
