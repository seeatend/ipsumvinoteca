Our Manifesto

 

At Ipsum Vinoteca we take Italian eating and drinking very seriously. Our aim is to give you the best possible experience, either you are having a glass of wine or a craft beer and sharing a platter with friends, or joining us for lunch or dinner in our intimate and cosy cellar room, sitting in one of the only 5 tables available surrounded by some of the best vintages and wines.

 

Ipsum Vinoteca has an outstanding wine list with more than 300 Italian wines and more than 30 available by the glass changed regularly. For this reason we have been awarded  "Best Wine list" at the Italian-English Awards, national competition reserved to all Italian establishments. Also we have a big selection of Italian craft beers and we mean it, no Peroni or Moretti, our own coffee, organic 80% fruit juices, artisan soft drinks. Our aim is to offer you the best available, we do not compromise or cut corners.

 

In our restaurant we use fresh, local ingredients together with some of the best Italian cheeses and charcuteries that come directly from Italy every week. Our house Balsamic vinegar is authentic from Modena and aged and our extra virgin olive oil is a DOP Tuscany and our food is made to order, this is why we only have 5 tables and a very short menu.

 

The idea behind our restaurant is not the menu changing daily, and we are the only restaurant in the country doing so, but cooking only what is fresh, in season and local, like our mums do,

but created by proper chefs. Our chefs go to the local market every morning in their quest for the best and freshest produces, we are not your typical Italian restaurant, we dont do pizzas and we rarely have pasta unless it is homemade.

 

Eating and drinking is a joy, a pleasure, cooking exceptional food or making great wine take time and effort, why eating or drinking in a rush. This is Ipsum Vinoteca and what we stand for.

Wine bar

At Ipsum vinoteca we offer an unmatched choice of Italian wines, with more than 30 by the glass, changed constantly, and about than 300 available by the bottle, from North to South, with plenty of old vintages, the biggest Italian wine list in the UK and are priced by adding a flat £15 to our retail prices as to offer you a great-value way to enjoy fantastic wines in a great setting.

We want you to have the best possible wine drinking experience, not only by offering you the biggest selection of Italian wines but providing you with the right setting and the right expertise behind the bar. All the wines on sale have been tasted by us and we love every single one, and to make wine more friendly, we have a great selection of wine flights, a selection of 3 different wines chosen by you or by us. Ipsum also stock one of the biggest selection of Italian craft beer with more than 30 available.

 

We also offer a selection of cold and hot dishes to accompany our wines, from cheese or charcuterie platters to soup and a collection of hot panini, all our food is made to order from our chefs and using high quality ingredients.

Shop

Ipsum is a wine shop but not as you know it. We stock over 300 Italian wines and keep adding on a daily base, we can proudly say that we have one of the biggest ranges in the UK and we aim to become the biggest. All our wines are from small, family owned vineyards located all over Italy, from Valle d’Aosta to the island of Pantelleria passing for Abruzzo and not available from supermarkets or off licences. We stock all the classics, from Pinot grigio to Prosecco, but plenty of unknown, unique wines.

 

 

We also sell other Italian specialties, from extra virgin olive oils to Modena Balsamic vinegar, from Grappas and Amaros to artisan beers, we have a big range of artisan beers available to take home.

We are passionate about Italy and Italian wines and have tasted every single wine we stock, so if you’d like some help choosing a wine, whether it is for a special occasion or simply to find to accompany your dinner, we will be happy to help and every day we have plenty of wines for your to taste before you buy.

 

We offer a bespoke gift service for all occasions, you decide the content and we do the rest.

 

 

Events

Part of the discovery journey is hosting regular tasting evenings guided by our sommeliers that know everything there is to know about the wine we stock and Italian wines, from regional to vertical tasting, and themed dinners. Ipsum will have plenty of events to suit all tastes and levels, from novice to professionals wine drinkers, from intimate get-togethers with top chefs, to exceptional feasts

 

At Ipsum, we run wine courses and our courses are a wonderful way to combine learning with pleasure in an informal yet fantastic setting. Courses are friendly and informative, suited for both novices and wine connoisseurs. Wine can seem daunting, but once you get to know it, you will love it.
