Restaurant

The restaurant, made only of 5 tables and by bookings only, offers a unique, gourmet experience. Our philosophy is using the best of what is fresh, in season and local, all our suppliers are no more than 30 miles from our kitchen, and our approach to food is amplify the flavours not cover them. All our dishes are freshly cooked. Our daily menu offers the option to have pairing wines for a truly unique experience. Our food cannot be defined Italian, however, it has the Italian cuisine at its heart.

Finally the wine list, our award winning wine list, that offers an outstanding selection of Italian wines, with wines to pair any food and any palate, with more than 300 labels, from sparkling to dessert wines, passing for red, white and rose.

The restaurant and bar

The restaurant offers a unique gourmet experience with a daily changing menu with a handful of dishes featuring the best, freshest and seasonal ingredients all sourced locally, within 30 miles from Leeds, freshly cooked with the option of having pairing wines for a truly unique experience. The restaurant is by booking only and is open 7 days a week. All our cheeses and charcuteries are imported directly from Italian small producers.

Our award winning wine bar is separate from the restaurant and is open all day every day and it offers from coffees to wine, from homemade pastries to platters and burgers and it offers in cooperation with Italyabroad.com, the biggest selection of Italian wine from small producers and Italian craft beers outside Italy, all available to drink at the bar or take home together with artisan Italian spirits, from gin to grappas, soft drinks and organic juices.

Our food

Our meat is 65 day matured in Himalayan Salt. Not “wetaged” in vac - packs to minimize weight loss. Not only our beef is aged longer but it comes from Ripon. 37 miles from us. From grass fed animals that graze freely in natural open pastures. By aging the meat that long the meat loses on weight costing us more but the ﬂavour and tenderness is dramatically improved. Our ﬁsh is always fresh and is delivered daily from a ﬁshmonger based in Harrogate. 15 miles from us. That brings us the freshest ﬁsh. We don't know what is coming until it is delivered. Our vegetables are bought daily from the Leeds market. All our cheeses and salami are directly imported from small Italian farms.

Finally our wine list goes hand in hand with our food and principles. We have put together an outstanding "Best Wine List” award winning all Italian. Small producers unknown labels and wines wine list with more than 300 wines and a big selection of Italian craft beers served both by the glass and by the bottle.
