Reservations

Ipsum Vinoteca brings together the vision of Andrea for exceptional food, his outstanding knowledge of Italian wine and beers and the very best and local ingredients skilfully prepared by our chefs. We pride ourselves of offering only the best available, without compromise

 

Our search for the best ingredients, from meat to fish, from cheeses to charcuterie, has been a fantastic journey

 

It’s a shame and very few are aware of it, but most British beef now comes from rapidly fattened on grain animals kept in close confinement and fed additives and hormones. Their meat is then either not aged or ‘wet-aged’ in vac-packs to minimize weight loss, all to make the process as quick and cheap as possible. All our meat comes from R&J in Ripon, North Yorkshire, we only use their signature range, where the animals are grass fed and graze freely in natural open pastures and our beef is matured in Himalayan Salt aged for at least 65 days  losing on weight, but improving the flavour and tenderness.

 

We decided to use a similar approach with our seafood, and instead of having "A"  fish on the menu and being forced to have it even when not at its best, we started working together with Ramus seafood in Harrogate,  that source the best seafood for us, and have a catch of the day, we don’t know what is coming until it actually arrives in the restaurant every morning, this way we know that it is the very best and freshest.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Lastly our cheeses and charcuterie. After numerous tasting trips to Italy (I know is a tough job but someone has to do it), we selected our suppliers, the search has not stopped and will never do.

 

As with all our ingredients, we only use the best and we know are not the cheapest, the best ingredients rarely are, but we think it’s definitely worth it.

 

Finally our wines and drinks. We decided to work together with Italyabroad.com, an Italian specialist importer and we have an outstanding all Italian wine list with more than 300 wines and were awarded  "Best Wine List" at the Italian-English Awards on our first year and a big selection of Italian craft beers.

At Ipsum vinoteca we offer an unmatched choice of all Italian wines, with more than 30 by the glass, changed constantly, and more than 300 available by the bottle, our wine list has been awarded “Best Wine List” at the Italian English Awards 2015 and we also offer one of the biggest selection of Italian craft beers in the UK.

 

All the wines have been tasted by us and we love every single one... and to make wine "friendlier", we have a great selection of wine flights, a selection of 3 different wines chosen by you or us to introduce you to the wonderful world of Italian wines.

 

We also offer a selection of cold and hot dishes to accompany our wines, from cheese or cold meat platters to Venison burger and sausages and sandwiches see menu...all our food is made to order from our chefs, nothing is pre made, so be patient and enjoy your drink.

Deposits and Cancellation Policy

For tables of 5 or more guests, we require a £10 per person non refundable deposit, which can be paid over the phone when making the reservation. (we will hold your table for 7 days from the date of booking until the full deposit has been paid.)

 

We require a 48-hour-notice for cancellations and amendments to number of guests. For parties of 10 or more, we require guests to pre-order from their chosen menu and advise the restaurant 48 hours in advance.

Ipsum Vinoteca is not just a wine shop, it is a library of Italian wines, we stock more than 300 different wines from all over Italy and add new ones constantly, we can proudly say that we have one of the biggest range in the UK and we aim to become the biggest! All our wines are from small, family-owned vineyards, from Valle d’Aosta to the island of Pantelleria and not available in supermarkets or off licences.
