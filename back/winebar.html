Winebar

Our wine bar, separate from the restaurant and open to everyone, not just our restaurant guests, offer an unmatched choice of Italian drinks, from wines to juices, all from small producers , with a selection of wine by the glass of more than 30 wines, constantly changed, and more than 300 available by the bottle, to drink at the bar, or take home. We have been awarded “best wine list” at the Italian English Awards 2015 and after our win, the category has been taken off. We also offer one of the biggest selection of Italian craft beers in the UK, if not the biggest, with over 70 beers available. But Ipsum is also a bar with a wide selection of Italian spirits, from gin to Grappa, and non alcoholic drinks from soft drinks to organic juices

Our bar, open 7 days a week, from morning to night, also offer a wide selection of food, from pastries to snacks, from platters to burgers and plenty of daily specials prepared by our chefs and food is served all day.

OUR MENU

Our wine bar menu is changed regularly, to reflect the seasons, and offers plenty of Italian classics, such pasta and risotto, and wine bar classic, such as burger and platters, and plenty of healthy and vegan and vegetarian options.
