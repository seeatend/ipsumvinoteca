Our Story

A UNIQUE DRINKING AND DINING EXPERIENCE

Ipsum Vinoteca is the creation of Andrea Dâ€™Ercole, chef patron, the realisation of a journey that started 20 years ago. Ipsum Vinoteca concept is clear, just very few tables, a short menu to ensure freshness, everything done in house, from marinated salmon to desserts, the best Italian wines and drinks from small producers, the very best local and seasonal ingredients, all freshly cooked for a unique experience, whether a glass of wine and a pasta or the whole 3 course meal, the aim is to provide the best experience possible to our guests, we want them to feel as they are with friends at home.

Located in an historic building just opposite the Victoria Quarter in Leeds, Ipsum Vinoteca is a bright, open space made of wood and stainless steel that reminds of the colors of the vines and the land, designed by an Italian architect. At Ipsum Vinoteca the emphasis is on the food and the people you are with, not on the surroundings.

Open 7 days a week, from morning to late night, Ipsum Vinoteca is a place for every time of the day and any occasion, from a morning espresso to an end of the day glass of wine

We are Italians and Italian at heart, and despite using fresh, seasonal and local produce, our style is Italian, we blend our history, traditions with what our local suppliers can offer us, reinterpret classic dishes and make them ours, for us, cooking is passion and love
