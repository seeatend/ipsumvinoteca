Restaurant

Our restaurant, made only of 5 tables and by bookings only is open 7 days a week and offers a unique, gourmet experience. Our philosophy is using only the best, the freshest, in season and local ingredients and our approach to food is to highlight the flavours, not to cover them. All our dishes are freshly cooked, carefully thought and presented. Our food cannot be defined Italian, however, it has the Italian approach to food at its heart.

Our restaurant has been designed so that your experience focuses on the food and the service, and allow you to spend time and enjoy the company you are with, and it starts with complimentary 48 hour fermented sourdough bread, Italian extra virgin olive oil and aged balsamic vinegar.

Our restaurant offers a unique experience with a daily changing menu with a handful of dishes, including vegetarian and vegan options, featuring the best, freshest and seasonal ingredients freshly cooked and for a full and total experience, our menu offers the option to have pairing wines.

Our food

All our food is sourced from small producers, Italian farms for cheese and charcuteries and specialties such as truffles and Buffalo mozzarella, and local for meat, fish and vegetables. Our meat comes from a farm in Ripon, our beef and lamb are aged in Himalayan salt for at least 65 days, grass fed animals, and we also work with them for extreme meat ageing, we love experimenting, currently our longest is 150days. Our fish is bought daily from the local market, just across the road from us, and our vegetable are all locally sourced and we do our best to use in season British vegetables. All our cheeses and charcuteries and specialties, being Italian, come directly from Italy, from small farms that we have visited and we have been working together since we started, we trust them and they know we respect their produce.

Finally our wine list goes hand in hand with our food and principles. We love wine and we love Italian wines.
